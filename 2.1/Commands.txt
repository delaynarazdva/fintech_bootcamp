vi a.txt # open vi text editor
    i for insert
    esc will take you to the bottom
        to save and exit :wq! and enter
        not save and exit :q! and enter
                        i  (insert mode, insert before cursor)
    esc (command mode)
    ZZ (save and exit)
    :wq (save and exit)
    :w (save without exiting)
    :q! (exit without saving)
    a (insert after cursor)
    o (insert a new line below)
    O (insert a new line above)
    x (delete one char)
    dw (delete one word)
    dd (delete the file)
    D (delete the rest of the line)
    u (undo the last action)
    h or left arrow (backward one char)
    l or right arrow (forward one char)
